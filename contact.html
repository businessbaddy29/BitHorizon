Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I open an account with BitHorizon?

Opening an account with BitHorizon is simple. Click on the "Register" button on our homepage, fill in your details, verify your email, and make your first deposit. The entire process takes just a few minutes.

What currencies can I trade on your platform?

BitHorizon offers trading on all major currency pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, etc.), minor pairs, and exotic pairs. We also provide access to cryptocurrencies, commodities, and indices.

Is my money safe with BitHorizon?

Yes, we take the security of your funds very seriously. We use bank-level encryption, keep client funds in segregated accounts, and are fully regulated and licensed in multiple jurisdictions.

How can I deposit and withdraw funds?

We offer multiple deposit and withdrawal methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and various e-wallets. Deposits are processed instantly, while withdrawals are typically processed within 24 hours.

Do you offer a demo account?

Yes, we offer a free demo account with virtual funds so you can practice trading strategies and familiarize yourself with our platform without risking real money.
